EASTERN AFRICA STATISTICAL TRAINING CENTER, THE 7th CONVOCATION

The Eastern Africa Statistical Training Center Invites All Students To Attend On The 7th Convocation Ceremony

TOPIC
The role of Official Statistics and Big Data in the economic transformation and sustainable development in Tanzania

THEME
Modernizing Data ecosystems to celebrate the implementation of Africa Continental Free Trade Area (AfCFTA):
The role of Official Statistics and Big Data in the economic transformation and sustainable development of Tanzania. Join us as we explore the transformative potential of data in shapping a brighter tomorrow for our nation.
